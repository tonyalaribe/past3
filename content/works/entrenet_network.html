Entrenet is a network marketting system with a goal to produce more financially free system.

Past 3 built a simple but functional Minimum Viable Product with react js and golang.

We built a customer facing website, dashboard for members and an admin dashboard for management.

From the dashboard, members are able to see and track their downlines via a genealogy tree, while also tracking the perks and money in their account. Admins on the other hand, are able to approve new registrations and track the system from their dashboard.
